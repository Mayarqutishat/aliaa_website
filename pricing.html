Frequently Asked Questions

Get Answers to Common Questions
What's the difference between the Basic and Premium plans? +

The Basic plan gives you access to our PDF workout plans and video course library, while the Premium plan includes all Basic features plus monthly live coaching sessions, customized workout plans, nutrition guidance, and progress tracking tools.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time? +

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your plan features until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer refunds? +

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact our support team within 14 days of your purchase for a full refund.

Are the workout programs suitable for beginners? +

Yes! We offer programs for all fitness levels, from complete beginners to advanced athletes. Our programs include modifications and progressions to suit your current fitness level.

How do I access the video courses and PDF programs? +

After purchasing a plan, you'll receive login credentials to access our member dashboard where all your programs, videos, and resources are available for streaming or download.
