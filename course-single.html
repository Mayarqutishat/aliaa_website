About This Course

This 15-minute High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) workout is specially designed for beginners who want to get fit without spending hours at the gym. Perfect for busy women and girls looking to incorporate effective exercise into their daily routine.

Our program focuses on full-body movements that boost metabolism, improve cardiovascular health, and build lean muscle. Each session is carefully crafted to be challenging yet achievable, with modifications available for all fitness levels.

As part of our commitment to supporting hijabi women in fitness, all demonstrations feature modest activewear, and we provide tips for comfortable exercise while wearing hijab.

Course Content

  • 1. Introduction & Warm-up 3 min
  • 2. Lower Body HIIT Circuit 4 min
  • 3. Upper Body Strength 3 min
  • 4. Core Activation 2 min
  • 5. Full Body Finisher 2 min
  • 6. Cool Down & Stretching 1 min
