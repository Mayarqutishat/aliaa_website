Welcome to Aliaa Fit

Empowering Women of All Ages Through Fitness

Aliaa Fit was created to provide a safe, supportive fitness community for women and girls aged 12-60, with special focus on the needs of hijabi women. We believe fitness should be accessible, comfortable, and aligned with your values.

Our platform combines modest sportswear, tailored training programs, and expert guidance to help you achieve your fitness goals whether at home or in the gym. We're committed to creating an inclusive environment where every woman can thrive.

Aliaa Hassan

Founder & Head Coach
